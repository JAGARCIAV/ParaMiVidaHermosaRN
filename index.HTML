To my love

Dear Bryan,

Loving you has brought so much light into my life. It is in the way you smile, the way you laugh, the way you look at me like I matter deeply, and even in the quiet moments that still make everything feel meaningful and warm.

I think about all the memories we have made, and I already treasure those little things. I can imagine that what matters most is not only the big moments, but also the simple ones: the talks, the glances, the laughs, and the everyday calm.

Even when life is not perfect, your presence feels special. You have a way of making everything feel softer, safer, and more beautiful. That is something I never want to take for granted.

So here is my promise: to keep choosing you, to keep caring, and to keep valuing what we share. Thank you for being someone who makes my heart feel at home.

Love,
Hazel
